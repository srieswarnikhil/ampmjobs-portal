Hello Users, Welcome to ampm jobs Application, this Application is a part time jobs platform A bridge connecting Students and Employers. This is a very user friendly application. It takes just 2 minutes to complete the registration process.

The Bridge helps Students and Employers connect and select each other depending upon their requirements.

The eligible Students should be above 18+ years. We help Students make some earning while they study for their better living and also become self sustained for their minimum needs. Students will have access to more part time job opportunities in multiple locations and domains of their choice, as we are building on the concept of “EARN WHILE YOU LEARN” across PAN India.

This Application allows Students to register online and search for part time jobs of their interest as all the vacancies available in our data base are listed based on the guide lines of registration.

Employers will have a platform where they can get a varied pool of talent from the registered Students. Employers will have access to Students who wish to take up part time jobs, to support eradicate child labour and have man power resources of their choice, available for their disposal at any point of time.

The Employers can post their requirements of part time jobs on the Application for a stipulated time, and also search for Students of their choice and line of activities. 

Our World Class ecosystem provides a new approach both for Students and Employers to build an upcoming work force

We have made this Application very user friendly, and the user interface is simple for both Students and Employers. As a principle we believe in very low and affordable registration process, which will enable both the Employers and the Students to come forward and get into the right direction of part time jobs concept.
